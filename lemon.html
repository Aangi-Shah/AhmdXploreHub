Lemon Tree Premier,The atrium

Lemon Tree Premier caters to a spectrum of needs with tailored services

About

On the bank of Sabarmati, overlooking the riverfront, lounges this plush and stylish hotel, enhanced by landscaped greens and a serene water body.Together with its personalized services, modern facilities and impeccable service standards,this hotel is a perfect place for the upbeat business and leisure traveler to interact and unwind.

The hotel is designed to attract the millennium traveler with its 63 spacious and well appointed rooms and suites, a multi-cuisine coffee shop– Citrus Café, with an interactive kitchen, and The Riverfront Grill which directly overlooks the Sabarmati river; besides a fully equipped gym and the largest indoor swimming pool in the city.

Key Features

Wake up to the picturesque view of the river from most rooms, trot at the joggers trail for a healthy start, enjoy sunrise over a hearty breakfast or end the day taking a leisurely walk by the riverside.
