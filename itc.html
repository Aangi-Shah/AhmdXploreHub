ITC Narmada

A Luxury Collection Hotel

About

Architecturally inspired by the stepwells of Gujarat and the traditional ‘toran’ gateways adorning its façade, ITC Narmada welcomes you with regal splendour. In keeping with the philosophy of ITC Hotels, ITC Narmada is a glowing celebration of the culture, arts and crafts of the region. A prime location and luxurious facilities make each stay memorable.

Undeniably luxurious, with décor that celebrates the vibrant arts and crafts of Gujarat, these beautiful rooms are instantly appealing. Hand-picked amenities and a marvellous ambience are backed by efficient service.

Key Features

Inspired by the erstwhile royal kitchens of India, this exclusive vegetarian restaurant offers a fine collection of timeless delicacies, based on the ancient Indian system of seasonal cooking & decades of culinary research.
