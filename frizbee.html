FrizBee

Ahmedabad's newest place to chill & hangout with your love ones.

About

With food stalls and exciting features like discotheque, VIP lounge, shopping & flea market, game zone, projection screen and live performance stage.

FrizBee is an exciting, high-energy spot that can be enjoyed by people of all ages and who do not mind a little running and a lot of fun.

Key Features

Frizbee is a great destination for families and friends to enjoy their weekends while enjoying quality food.
