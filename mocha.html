Mocha

A decent place with some good ambiance

About

Mocha is the country's first completely indigenous and eclectic café chain, known not just for its menu, but the varied experiences it brought to 20 outlets in 18 cities to expand the cafe culture.

In addition to shopping, Ahmedabad One Mall features entertainment options such as a multiplex cinema, creating a well-rounded experience for visitors. The mall is known for its modern architecture, spacious interiors, and a vibrant atmosphere that attracts locals and tourists alike.

Key Features

Mocha has influenced the world-view of an entire generation, to stir a social revolution.
