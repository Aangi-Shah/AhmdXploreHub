Under The Neem Tree

A beautiful place to treat yourself to a sumptuous meal

About

Under the Neem Trees, a true twin multi-cuisine restaurant that serves delicious culinary delights from round the globe. It has twin restaurants parting from the beautiful hallway.

The Neem Chowk, a place with rich Gujarati touch to it. It serves authentic Gujarati cuisine and its ambiance is inspired by the rich heritage of the city..The Neem County, a luxe multicuisine restaurant. It offers an exquisite dining experience.

Key Features

Gastronome’s reverie comes alive once you are at Neem Dream! …where hand-crafted dishes take the center-stage.
