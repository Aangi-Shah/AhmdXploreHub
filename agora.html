Agora Mall

High-end Destination

About

The Shree Balaji Agora Mall, a commercial shopping and entertainment mall in Ahmedabad, is a part of the Shree Balaji Group of Companies. It has a great number of Anchor Shops as well as small Retail Outlets, a 4-Screen Multiplex, Kids Gaming Zone, Restaurants, a Bowling Alley, Food Court and more.

The entire mall is centrally air-conditioned and is made beautiful with lovely landscapes, huge fountains and a well-architected contemporary structure. .

Key Features

They entertain you not only with play and game zones but also lovely food served at roof-top restaurants.
